Surveys & schemes / ASUSE

When it started

ASUSE was launched in 2021–22, replacing the earlier NSS enterprise survey series, to provide annual rather than periodic estimates of India's unincorporated sector.

Motive of the survey

Most small manufacturing units, shops and service businesses in India aren't registered companies, so they fall outside corporate filings entirely. ASUSE exists to measure this large, otherwise invisible part of the economy every year instead of once every five or six years.

What is analysed

Number of enterprisesBy sector — manufacturing, trade, services.
Employment generatedWorkers engaged, including unpaid family labour.
Gross value addedEconomic output of the unincorporated sector.
Formalisation indicatorsRegistration, bank accounts, digital payment use.
