Shimla RO and its sub-regional offices

One headquarters coordinates field operations delegated to three sub-regional offices, each responsible for a cluster of districts.

Headquarters

Shimla RO

Overall coordination, Deputy Director level oversight of all survey operations in Himachal Pradesh.

 Sub-office

SRO Hamirpur

Covers Hamirpur, Una and Bilaspur districts.

 Sub-office

SRO Dharamshala

Covers Kangra and Chamba districts.

 Sub-office

SRO Mandi

Covers Mandi, Kullu and Lahaul-Spiti districts.

District coverage shown above is indicative — confirm against the official MoSPI jurisdiction order before publishing.
