Agricultural & Rural Labour Wage Surveys (AL–RL)

FrequencyPeriodic wage rounds
ScopeAgricultural & rural occupations
Surveys & schemes / AL–RL

Motive of the survey

Wage rates for agricultural and rural labour are set locally and change with season and crop cycle, so there's no single administrative record of what workers actually earn. This survey collects wage rates directly from the field to track how rural earnings move over time.

What is analysed

Daily wage ratesBy occupation — ploughing, sowing, harvesting and more.
Agricultural vs non-agriculturalComparison across rural occupation types.
Seasonal variationHow wages shift with the crop calendar.
Gender-wise wage gapsDifferences in rates paid to men and women.
