When it started

AIDIS is conducted roughly once a decade as part of the NSS round cycle, with the most recent round run alongside the 77th Round. The long gap between rounds is deliberate — household debt and asset positions shift slowly enough that decennial measurement captures the real trend.

Motive of the survey

Bank records only show formal borrowing. A large share of rural and low-income household debt comes from moneylenders, relatives and informal sources that never appear in any financial institution's books. AIDIS exists to capture the complete picture, formal and informal together.
