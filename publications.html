What these surveys actually measure

Plain-language explainers, aimed at students, researchers and journalists rather than statisticians.

What is PLFS?

A household survey that asks people about their work in the past week and year, used to estimate how many people are employed, unemployed, or outside the labour force.

What does DTES capture?

How much domestic tourists spend, where they go, and how long they stay — used to size the state's tourism economy.
